# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@shipsite.dev**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be removed from the project.
